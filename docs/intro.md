# Welcome to the Humanoid Robotics Book

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on humanoid robotics! This book will take you on a journey through the fascinating world of physical artificial intelligence and humanoid robot development.

## About This Book

This book is structured into six comprehensive chapters, each building upon the previous to give you a complete understanding of humanoid robotics:

1. **Introduction to Physical AI** - Foundational concepts of AI integrated with physical systems
2. **Foundations of Robotics** - Core principles of robotics, systems, and mechanisms
3. **Human-Inspired Design Principles** - How human biology inspires robot design
4. **Perception Systems** - How robots sense and understand their environment
5. **AI, Deep Learning & Control Systems** - The brain and nervous system of robots
6. **Humanoid Locomotion and Manipulation** - How robots move and interact with the world
